Gardening Tips & Resources

Monthly Tips for August

  • Plant Fall Vegetables: Start planting cool-weather crops like kale, spinach, and broccoli for a fall harvest.
  • Prune and Deadhead: Keep your garden looking tidy by pruning overgrown shrubs and deadheading spent flowers
  • Mulch: Add a layer of mulch to retain moisture and suppress weeds during the hot summer months.
  • Check for Pests: Inspect your plants regularly for signs of pests and treat them promptly using eco-friendly methods.
